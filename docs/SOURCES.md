# Sources & Traditions

## Our Approach

This app synthesizes interpretations from **multiple Gita traditions** — classical and modern — into original, accessible content. We do NOT rely on any single translation. The Sanskrit text is public domain; our synthesis, applications, and life-question mappings are original.

## Verse Numbering Standard

We use the **standard 700-verse, 18-chapter** structure (used by virtually all editions including Gita Press, academic publishers, and traditional commentators). This is not specific to any one publisher — it is the universally accepted chapter/verse numbering.

## What's Original to This App

1. **Life applications** across 4 pillars (Personal Growth, Career, Health, Relationships)
2. **Life questions** — real questions people ask that each verse answers
3. **Multi-tradition synthesis** — combining classical perspectives into accessible prose
4. **Thematic tagging** — connecting verses to modern concerns
